This site was made with entirely with vanilla JavaScript, without the assistance of jQuery or any other JS frameworks / CSS frameworks. GSAP, ScrollMagic and ChartJS were used for basic transitions and creating the charts.

This project was designed to force me to practice DOM manipulation / responsive web development without the assistance of anything besides vanilla JavaScript, and also helped familuarize me with all the candidates in the 2020 democratic feild.

This was developed in January 2020, so current candidates and polling might not be up to date.
